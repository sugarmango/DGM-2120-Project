Learn About Our Programs

Our fun interactive programs include access to equipped labs including Little Scientist© coats, safety glasses, and gear so they can get more of a real feel of being a little scientist.

What we offer:

  • STEAM Programs
  • Robotics
  • Creative Coding
  • Video Game Design
  • Astronomy
  • Paleontology
  • Mathletes
  • Early Foundations for the littler ones

Please schedule a tour with us and learn more about how you can contribute to your children's early STEAM education and develop a lifetime love for science and real magic.
